Museum of Useless & Random Items

A creation of Stephan, Che, Tom, Hiro & Steve

The Creation

U&R then, now and its first visitors.

Officially opened to the public since October 2021, the Museum of Useless and Random Items was the Team's idea to deal with excessive unrelated and useless objects that accumulated in Apartment 16. Originally featuring only a dozen pieces, the Museum has expanded drastically and is now home to over 70 items with no purpose including a collection of leaves and herbs, various pine cones and a garden with self-sustaining ecosystems. Our first visitor, Mr Thanh Tung Tran, arrived on 22.10.2021.

The Creator

Credit: Bobby Dang

The creator of the Museum, nicknamed "The Creator" or sometimes also Stephan, Che, Tom, Hiro & Steve is a student of the TU Dortmund. He is well-known for his abysmal intelligence, his life of pure childish fascinations and his reputation for making explosions and fires.

Our Philosophy

Simplicity, creativity, enthusiasm.

Here at the Museum, we value simplicity, creativity and enthusiasm. In many ways, our artworks are the embodiment of our principles. They also provide great insights into stages of the Creators life, his beliefs and his passions.
