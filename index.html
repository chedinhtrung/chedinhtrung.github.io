The Biosphere

The Biosphere is a smart terrarium controlled using MQTT over the internet with an Android application. It monitors temperature, humidity, CO2 level, and the lights and fogger can also be controlled from the app. I can even make it rain and flash lightning in there.

The Tesla Coil

The Tesla Coil is a high voltage device invented by Nikola Tesla. This modern implementation works on the same principles, but uses MOSFETs for switching. It can light up bulbs wirelessly and even play music from its arcs.
